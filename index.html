Hi Guys,


Let's get some information out of the way: my name is Adam Cox, I have a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from Northern Illinois University, and I am a 2015 graduate of Code Louisville.


As an artist I work primarily in drawing, printmaking, and collage. My art background compliments my web development skills, as I focus a lot on the visual design and layout of the sites I work on. I use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on my sites, but also have a strong working knowledge of other languages, frameworks, and software, including jQuery, Bootstrap, and Adobe Photoshop. When I'm not coding or making art, I'm usually helping organize and manage regional and local rollerblading events.


My years spent working on these organizational committees has taught me a lot about cooperation and the benefits of working as a team to achieve a common goal, but also the importance of thinking outside the box. I always strive to find creative ways to approach and solve problems, and I want to continue to learn, grow and explore. I know that I can bring new ideas to the table and inspire the team to break the mold.
