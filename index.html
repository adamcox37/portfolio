Hi Guys,


Let get some information out of the way: my name is Adam Cox, I am a graduate of Northern Illinois University with a bachelors’ in Fine Arts, and I am also graduate of Code Louisville. I’ve been working with front end coding for quite some time, I’m an artist working primarily in drawing, printmaking, and collage, and also I lend a helping hand out for regional rollerblading events and heavily assist with management of more local rollerblading events. My web development skills go hand and hand with my art background where I focus a lot on the visual design and layout of the sites I work on utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to achieve this goal. I also have a strong working knowledge with other languages, frameworks, and software, these include jQuery, Bootstrap, and Adobe Photoshop.


My years spent working with committees organizing rollerblading events has taught me a lot when it comes to working as team in either an in-person setting or spread out in more of a global scope. At the same time I understand the importance putting in my effort to push the project along to meet either deadlines or general goals set up for success. I strive on creative thinking that leads to innovation, discovering a different way to solve problem is something I take pride in. It is something that keeps me on my toes and helps in keeping myself sharp and up-to-date.


I want to grow, I want to learn, I want to explore. I am the person who can bring new ideas and inspire the team to crack the mold and approach a situation from a new direction.
